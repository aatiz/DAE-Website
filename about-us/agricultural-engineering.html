What is Agricultural Engineering?

Agricultural Engineering is basically application of engineering in agricultural production, processing and utilization. When we think of infrastructure development in agriculture, it is application of civil engineering and when we think of agri-mechanization, it is application of mechanical engineering.

Recently electrical engineering ,electronics engineering, robotics and artificial intelligence are also finding applications in agriculture production, processing and utilization. That way ,scope of agricultural engineering is widening day by day.

Agri processing engineers can be called agro-industry engineers. That way, agricultural engineers are linked up with industries too. When we talk of utilization, it is generally food industries.From harvesting to clean grain production, it may come under the preview of processing but from grain to flour milling and still further to the production of noodles, biscuits and bakeries ,it may fall under the arena of food processing.

Agricultural Engineers as food processing engineers are more fitting than any other branches of engineering. Agricultural Engineers are capable of working as rural engineers to develop rural infrastructures, harness water and solar power for rural energy development. Agricultural Engineers are increasingly involved in developing robots for 3D agricultural operations-1st D for dirty, 2nd D for difficult and 3rd D for dangerous operations.

Agricultural Engineers can specialize in irrigation, land & water, agri machinery & power, agri procssing & post-harvest, food processing, hydro-power ,energy, construction management and even structural engineering.

Agricultural Engineers are more misunderstood than understood. Employers may hire them for any job of engineering nature in industries,infrastructure development, research & development preferably in food, agriculture, forestry, agro-forestry and even renewable energy development.

In short agricultural engineers are called agri or agro engineers. This profession is interdisciplinary in nature meaning that they ‘ve to compete with civil engineers ,mechanical engineers and even environmental engineers when they’ve to enter into any job.
